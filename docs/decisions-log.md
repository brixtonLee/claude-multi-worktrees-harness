# Decisions Log

> **Purpose:** Record of architectural and design decisions with rationale.
> This prevents re-debating settled decisions and gives future-you context
> on WHY things are the way they are.
> Claude: Append here when making any non-trivial design choice.

---

## Format

```
### DEC-[NNN]: [Decision Title]
**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Context:** [What problem or question prompted this decision]
**Decision:** [What was decided]
**Rationale:** [Why this option was chosen over alternatives]
**Alternatives Considered:**
- [Alt 1] — rejected because [reason]
- [Alt 2] — rejected because [reason]
**Consequences:** [What this decision implies for future work]
```
